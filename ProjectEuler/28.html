Project Euler Problem 28

Description

Starting with the number 1 and moving to the right in a clockwise direction a 5 by 5 spiral is formed as follows: 

21 22 23 24 25
20  7  8  9 10
19  6  1  2 11
18  5  4  3 12
17 16 15 14 13

It can be verified that the sum of the numbers on the diagonals is 101.

What is the sum of the numbers on the diagonals in a 1001 by 1001 spiral formed in the same way?

Solution

从1开始顺时针生成列表，参照上面的例子，当最终生成的列表大小为1001*1001时，求该列表对角线上所有数的和。

观察可得，当列表为n*n时（n为奇数且大于1），其右上角的值为n*n，左上角为n*n-(n-1)，左下角为n*n-2(n-1)，右下角为n*n-3(n-1)，则最外层对角线4个数的和为4*n*n-6*(n-1)。

Code

#!/usr/bin/python3
# -*- coding: utf-8 -*-

import math
import re
from timeit import Timer

def the_sum_of_numbers_on_the_diagonals(n):
    res = 0
    for i in range(3, n+1, 2):
        res += 4 * i * i - 6 * (i - 1)
    print(res+1)

if __name__ == "__main__":
    the_sum_of_numbers_on_the_diagonals(1001)
